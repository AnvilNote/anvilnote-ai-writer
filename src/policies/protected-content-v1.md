# Protected content policy v1

AnvilNote protected placeholders represent formulas, code, URLs, identifiers, or other content that must survive a rewrite exactly.

- Preserve every protected placeholder verbatim, including its braces, spelling, case, and digits.
- Do not translate, rewrite, delete, duplicate, split, or merge a placeholder.
- Do not reorder placeholders unless the surrounding metadata explicitly says that order may change.
- Do not create a new placeholder.
- Do not guess or describe the original content represented by a placeholder.
- Return all placeholders exactly once. AnvilNote will reject the result rather than guess when validation fails.
